Vegtable Rice

This rice dish, contains only vegetables and is therefore vegan. It is intended as a warm meal for the day, but can also be eaten cold. The portion is larger, because it should fill an active person.

  • 150g rice
  • two garlic cloves
  • sesame oil
  • two paprika
  • one obergine
  • one zucchini
  • one onion
  • parsley
  • salt
  1. cook the rice
  2. add sesame oil into a pan and heat it
  3. sauté onion
  4. add the cut paprika, obergine and zucchini
  5. when they are soft add some more sesame oil and the pressed garlic
  6. after five minutes add the cooked rice and salt it
  7. mix well and leave to infuse for 10 min on low heat

Breakfast Shake

Description

This shake is for after your morning workout. It contains vitamins and important fiber, for a good recovery. It can also be taken as a meal in between.

Ingredients

  • 250g lean curd
  • 20g flaxseed or oil
  • 40-50g oatmeal
  • 100g fruit
  • 250ml carrot juice

Steps

  1. pour carrot juice into the mixer
  2. add lean curd
  3. add the flaxseed
  4. throw in the oatmeal
  5. mix at a low level
  6. add some fruit
  7. mix until liquid

Spinach in puff pastry

Spinach in puff pastry is a delicious appetizer or main dish. With cheese, this can already make properly full. Of course, you can also distort this dish hot and cold.

  • finished puff pastry
  • spinach
  • goat cheese
  1. preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. spread the puff pastry on a baking sheet
  3. spread the spinach lengthwise and add the cheese
  4. fold the whole thing together and put in the oven for 25 min
